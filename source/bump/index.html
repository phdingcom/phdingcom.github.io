Bump!

I'm not feeling like working on this lovely Friday night so I did two things to my blog:

  • I transferred my domain from Godaddy to Google Domains, enlonging the validality to 2016 ($12/yr, while Godaddy intended to charge me CNY94.99/yr after the first year)
  • I transferred my web hosting from Bluehost to Github page, because bluehost rejected my request of configuring git on its shared host (and cPanel just does not suit the workflow of Jekyll & Octopress)

Ideally this change should be transparent to you -- you should feel like you're looking at the same site. But since this is the first time I deal with such transfer, if you see something not working as before, please let me know.

And the good news is that after the change, I'm much likely to keep updating than before. Good workflow does make work much more enjoyable, doesn't it?
